Welcome to

The Chain

A game to test your musical abilities!

Open your ears and let the fun begin!

Game rules:

  • Press "Go" to start the game

  • The game will then start to play a sequence of notes.

  • When the sequence is over, try and remember them and play them again using the keys!

  • For each sequence that you are correct, the game will increase the difficulty and add one more key to the sequence.

  • You will get a score for each correct sequence and your goal is to make to level 10!
